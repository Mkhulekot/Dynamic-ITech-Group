Who Are We ?

Dynamic  ITech  Group 

We 'Dynamic ITech Group' are the generation of new technologies, a division of the DIT Group established in the year 2021. We have operations in the Gauteng and Mpumalanga provinces with focus on government departments, state entities and privately owned companies. The division is lead by young qualified IT profesionals with university qualifications. We aim to train 5 000 young people and create job opportunities by 2030 for south african young profesionals wishing to grow in the IT field. Our main operational focus is in:
